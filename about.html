Mukono Kings High School is registered and licensed by the Ministry of Education and Sports under the Reg. No PSS/M/101 with the UNEB Center Number U/1595. Discipline is a forefront aspect and a core qualification of every student joining Mukono Kings High School. At Mukono Kings High School, we uplift talents of students through different skills like in Games, Sports, Drama etc.

Our history
The school was started by Mr. SSETTUBA ROBERT grounded on religious values to uplift the education system not only in Mukono and the surrounding areas but also in the entire country. Over the years, the school has evolved to be one of the best school in Academics and Sports and it is the District Champion for the Boys' secondary schools Football league, the Girls' foot ball Champions for Greater Mukono region.
